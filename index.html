Recently, I realized that every morning I was feeling incomplete, because I wasn't seeing enough chicken pictures.

I knew that I wasn't alone in this craving, so I decided to create Chicken A Day.

If you sign up, every morning you will wake up to a marvelous chicken picture in your inbox that has been selected with care by my automated system from the endless reserve of chicken pictures on Flickr.

Sign up, and go to bed every night secure in the knowledge that there will be a chicken waiting for you in the morning (or some other time, depending on where you live).
